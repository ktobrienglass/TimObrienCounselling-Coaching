About Me

Warm, confidential support that meets you where you are

Services

My aim is to help people find peace and comfort within themselves so they can live the life they want. People come to therapy for many reasons. The world can seem uncaring and hostile. They may have encountered stress, loss or trauma in their life. I provide a safe, confidential space where you can talk openly without judgement. I am here to help you, and that’s our focus.

More about me

I’ve always worked in helping professions; initially as a Youth Worker. I worked in England, Germany and Cyprus, supporting young people through the transition of adolescence. I later became Head of Youth Services in one of London’s most deprived boroughs, and subsequently managed Services for Children and Families in Kent.

My passion is helping individuals get the best out of life, and I believe counselling is the most effective way for me to do this.
